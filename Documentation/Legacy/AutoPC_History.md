# AutoPC History (Legacy)

AutoPC was an early naming layer used during proof-of-concept stages.

## Current Status
- AutoPC is deprecated as an active platform identity.
- Hecateon is the canonical platform name.
- Remaining references are historical or migration-only.

## Migration Notes
- Runtime and launch pipeline consolidated under Hecateon naming.
- Legacy launcher names remain as temporary shims with deprecation notices.
- Namespace and module consolidation continues in controlled refactor phases.
